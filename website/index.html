Features

Everything is built around discovery and direct contact — we do not run bookings, contracts, or payroll for domestic work inside the app.

Simple sign-in

Create an account with your name and mobile number, then verify via SMS (one-time code). No social login required — fewer steps for helpers and families.

Helper profiles

Helpers publish a photo, services, and rates (30 min / 1 hr / 2 hr). Families see clear pricing before they call or message.

Nearby discovery

When you allow location, listings can be shown by distance so you find people who work in your area. Location is optional and controlled in your device settings.

Reviews & ratings

Read feedback from other families to shortlist helpers. Leave your own rating after contact where the flow supports it.

Call & WhatsApp

Contact details open your phone dialer or WhatsApp so you agree timing, scope, and pay directly with the helper — the way many households already work.

Optional plans

Free tiers cover light use. Premium (families) and Maid Pro (helpers) add higher limits — see plans & pricing.

Why this app matters

Hiring home help is personal: trust, clarity on rates, and a respectful way to connect matter. Find My Maid is designed to reduce friction on both sides without pretending to replace real-world agreements.

  • For families: compare helpers in one place, see distances and prices, and reach out when you are ready — without a middleman charging per placement inside the app.
  • For helpers: present your services and rates honestly, appear in local discovery, and receive contacts according to fair lead limits unless you upgrade to Maid Pro.
  • For transparency: we are a discovery tool only. Employment, safety, and payment for work stay between you and comply with your local laws — we describe this clearly in our Terms.
  • For dignity: technology should make it easier — not harder — for domestic workers and households to find each other on clear terms.

How it works

1 · Create a profile

Helpers add a photo, rates for 30 min / 1 hr / 2 hr, and services. Families sign up in seconds.

2 · Discover nearby

Sort by distance, compare prices, and read ratings before you reach out.

3 · Contact directly

Call or WhatsApp — no in-app booking. Stay safe: meet in trusted places and agree terms clearly.

Subscriptions

Free tiers include limited contacts (families) and limited monthly leads (helpers). Premium and Maid Pro remove those limits. Final prices are shown in the app in Indian Rupees (INR) before you confirm purchase on the App Store or Google Play.
