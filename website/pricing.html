Plans & pricing

The app is free to download. You can use core features at no charge, within the free limits below. Premium (for families) and Maid Pro (for helpers) are optional upgrades you buy inside the app through Apple or Google.

The numbers here are shown in INR as a guide. At checkout, App Store and Google Play show exact local tax and final billing.

What’s included for free

Everyone starts here — no subscription required.

For families

Browse helpers, read profiles and reviews, and reach out by phone or WhatsApp.

3 free contacts, lifetime

After that, Premium lets you contact as many helpers as you like while your plan is active.

For helpers

List your photo, services, and rates, show up in discovery, and get enquiries from families.

3 incoming leads per month

Maid Pro removes the monthly cap so you can receive unlimited leads while subscribed.

Currency & billing region

Choose how estimates look on this page. The app Premium screen has the same control. Stores still bill in your locale at checkout.

Optional subscriptions

Choose exactly what you prefer inside the app — monthly or yearly.

For families

Premium

Unlimited contacts with helpers for as long as you stay subscribed.

  • No more limit after your first three free contacts
  • Same discovery and calling experience — just more reach
Rs 199 per month
Rs 1499 per year — saves vs paying monthly
For helpers

Maid Pro

Aimed at helpers who want steady visibility without a monthly lead cap.

  • Unlimited incoming leads each month while subscribed
  • Your profile and rates stay in your control
Rs 299 per month
Rs 2299 per year — lower than twelve months at the monthly rate

Manage or cancel

Open Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad (Apple ID), or in the Google Play Store on Android. There you can turn off renewal, see receipts, or request help from Apple or Google. We cannot see your full card details — the store handles payment.
