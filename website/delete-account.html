Delete your account

Effective date: 7 May 2026

You can request deletion of your Find My Maid account and the data associated with it at any time. This page explains what gets deleted, what may be retained for legal reasons, and exactly how to send the request.

1. Who can request deletion

Anyone with a Find My Maid account — whether registered as a family user or as a helper — can request deletion. You do not need to be signed in to the app to send the request, but you must verify the phone number registered with the account so we can confirm ownership.

2. How to request deletion

  1. Send an email from any address to support@findmymaid.online.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my FindMyMaid account.
  3. In the email body include:
    • Registered phone number (with country code, e.g. +91XXXXXXXXXX)
    • Registered name
    • Account type — family or helper
    • Reason (optional)
  4. We will reply within 2 business days to confirm receipt and verify ownership (we may send an SMS code to your registered phone number).
  5. Once verified, your account and associated data are removed within 7 business days.

Email support to delete account

3. What gets deleted

  • Your profile: display name, phone number, profile photo, and account role.
  • Your helper listing (if applicable): rates, services, location coordinates, verification status.
  • Your authentication records (one-time-password history with our SMS provider).
  • Reviews and ratings you authored (subject to legal retention).
  • Any support history linked to your account, on request.

4. What may be retained

For legal, security, and fraud-prevention reasons we may retain a limited amount of information after deletion:

  • Anonymised usage logs (no longer linked to your identity) for up to 12 months for service-quality and abuse-prevention purposes.
  • Abuse reports filed against the account, if any, to keep other users safe.
  • Subscription/transaction records processed by Apple App Store or Google Play, where retention is required by tax or accounting law. These records are held by the respective store and not under our control.

These limited records cannot be used to identify you and are deleted on schedule.

5. Cancellation of subscriptions

Deleting your Find My Maid account does not automatically cancel any in-app subscription you bought through Apple App Store or Google Play. To cancel a subscription, manage it in your App Store / Google Play account settings. See Pricing for details.

6. Re-creating an account later

After deletion you can sign up again with the same phone number, but you will start with a fresh empty profile — old data is not restored.

7. Contact

Questions about deletion: email support@findmymaid.online or visit Support / Contact.
